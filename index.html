ABOUT US

Vizag Pigeon Sports Society is the newly formed friendly club in the city of Visakhapatnam.Our club was formed on 1st february 2017 with lots of blessings by the old fanciers and our well wishers.It is our group achievement to reach a race of 780 kms in our first year of formation.Crossed many hurdles with support of all aour fellowship.We, the members of VPSS strive hard to promote this sport and brotherhood all along the coast of Vizag.

Vizag Pigeon Sports Society have peoples from different work background .We have doctors,graduates,politicians and businessmen.We all are completely friendly,cooperative and ideas sharing group helping the youngsters to develop their own skills.We are really thankful to each and everyone who has supported us to reach this far and our club needs more and more like you.
